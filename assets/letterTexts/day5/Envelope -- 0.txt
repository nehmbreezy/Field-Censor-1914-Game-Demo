IMPORTANT UPDATE FOR FIELD CENSORS
New regulations are in place to make the postal process more efficient and prevent unauthorised mail.
Tasks
4. Reject letters with INCOMPLETE Return Information on the envelope. Letters must include the following information on the soldier’s envelope:
—His Rank                      		
—Full Name of Soldier     		
—Brigade AND Division Name
Exemplar Address:
—Private Mike Hunt
—47th Infantry Reserve, 8th Division
To reject a letter, do not stamp it with your Field Censor Approval Stamp when you submit it.
NOTE: Even if you intend to reject a letter, you CENSOR IT ANYWAY. Sometimes the Post Service will fill in the missing details of the sender and deliver it to the intended recipient.
